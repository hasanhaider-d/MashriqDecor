About Mashriq Decor

At Mashriq Decor, we craft premium metal and wooden decor blending timeless artistry with modern elegance. Based in Moradabad, UP, our roots go back to 1952. From a boutique start to global exports, we partner with artisans using eco-friendly materials and traditional techniques. Each piece reflects passion for quality, sustainability, and the beauty of handcrafted design.
